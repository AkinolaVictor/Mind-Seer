This is Mind Seer, an app that reaveals the word the user has in mind.

Click To Play

HOW TO PLAY

  1. 1. Think of a Word within you.
  2. 2. Count the number of letters it has(i.e. get the length of the word).
  3. 3. Two questions would be asked about each letter in the word
  4. 4. Answer all these Questions
  5. 5. Click on reveal word to confirm the word
